Mulch Laying

We cover all your mulch needs and give plenty of options while we're at it. No matter if you want affordable or premium, or anything in between, Evan's has you covered. We also offer you a choice between having the mulch delivered for you to lay yourself or we can professionally lay it for you to ensure your yard looks the best it can.



Mulch Qualities

  • Black Forest

    Premium color enhanced mulch, dark black color, mixed bark and hardwoods.

  • Midnight Supreme

    Premium organic mulch, triple shredded, dark brown, mixed bark and hardwoods.

  • Wood Chips

    Best suited for ground cover away from the house, unpaved driveways, mulch piles, pathways, or parking areas
